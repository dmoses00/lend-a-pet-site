Hi! I'm Lydia, an expert pet sitter on Long Island.

I am a professional pet sitter based in Hicksville, New York. My family and I are all huge animal lovers. My team is my family and we offer pet sitting, dog walking and pet transfer. I have grown up with animals my entire life.

I have also volunteered for Forgotten Friends, and have also been very active with spaying and neutering countless feral cats, with the TNR program.

I will take care of your precious fur babies while you are away. Your pet will be a part of our family, and will have free roam of our house and fenced in yard. They will have ample opportunity for exercise or just plain relaxing on the deck. They will go on walks and be lavished with attention and affection.I will try to fulfill any of your pets special needs, in order to ensure the best possible experience. I'm available weekdays, most weekends, and can pet sit in my home or yours. If you choose for your pet to stay at home, I can come over twice or three times a day to feed and walk your dog. I look forward to meeting all your wonderful fur babies.
